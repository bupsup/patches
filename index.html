FAQs

  • How much is shipping?

Mailing cost is INCLUDED in price, unless you're bulk ordering or want special shipping. All prices are in USD.

  • Do you ship to x?

Yes. If you have a functioning national mail service, I will mail to your country.

  • How do I order?

Email me.

  • How can I pay?

Paypal, Venmo, GPay, or a credit card transaction. Up to you. Credit card will add a small (Usually <$1) fee.

  • Do you accept trades?

Sure, email me.

  • Velcro?

No patches have velcro, but I'll manually attach some for $3 if you're interested.

  • Hello? Is this still active?

I am still alive as of May 2023.

  • Can I trust you with my home address?

I don't care where you live. I don't store/track any of your information.

  • Will you give me the design file?

Probably not, sorry.

  • Can I use your design on something else?

I do have copyright on my designs, but I'm happy to chat or help design something with you.

  • Do you do this professionally?

Some design, yes, but not patches. I just like patches and made the ones I wanted to have. I make very little money doing this. So far, all profits have gone to funding the next run of patches. It's a sort of pyramid scheme with myself.

  • Anything else to know?

If you chat with me or send me a pic, I may quote you here or post your photo. If you have a problem with that, just let me know and I can remove or update it. I do appreciate seeing the patches out there, so feel free to send some photos.
